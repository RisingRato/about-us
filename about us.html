The Rising Rato Story

Welcome to Rising Rato, a bold new name in Kathmandu's fashion scene.

Founded on January 1st, 2025, by Aadarsh Khatri — a 17-year-old visionary with a passion for creativity and marketing — Rising Rato was born from a simple dream: to deliver high-quality, stylish matching clothes that let you express who you are, together.

From street corners to home deliveries, we're redefining how fashion meets everyday life. We're not just a clothing brand — we're a movement of young creators bringing freshness, authenticity, and confidence to the streets of Kathmandu.

Though we started with clothes, Rising Rato is not stopping there. We're just getting started. In the near future, expect new collections, exciting product lines, and even more ways to wear your vibe.

We currently deliver across Kathmandu with Cash on Delivery (COD) for maximum convenience. And as we grow, online payments and nationwide delivery are coming soon.

Follow our journey, join the family, and let's rise together — this is Rising Rato.
